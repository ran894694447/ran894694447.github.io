Jiangfan Ran

Jiangfan Ran is a master's student majoring in mechanical engineering from the School of Automation, Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, advised by Prof.Haibin Yan. He received his BS degree in electrical engineering from the School of information engineering, Henan University of science and technology, Luoyang, in 2022. His main research areas are computer vision and intelligent robotics.
